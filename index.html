About Me

Back in 2019, I decided to build a webpage for an organization that I started at my university called the African Student Association. I wanted to showcase the new officers, upcoming events, provide newsletters and connect new members with a one-stop-shop for all things ASA. From there, I began building websites as a side hobby while pursuing a Bachelors of Science in Biochemistry. Although I was on the pre-med track, I still found the time to hone my skills and upon graduating I landed a job at A Cloud Guru - Pluralsight. There, I was able to get hands on learning about the cloud and enterprise sales. Fast-forward to today, I'm working at Oracle Netsuite and enrolled in UT Austin's Coding bootcamp to further my web development skills that will aid in landing a technical role in the IT space.
